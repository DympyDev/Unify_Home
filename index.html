Endless Home

Endless Home is a home screen replacement for android focused of the limits, which are none. With regular home screen replacements you place your apps and widgets on a screen, but you are limited to a grid and the number of screens. When you use Endless Home you have the option to add as many items as you want on as many screen as you want, the screen will simply become scrollable so all your content will fit.
